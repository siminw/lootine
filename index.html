About

Lootine is a habit-tracking app that makes building daily routines fun. Complete your tasks, earn points, and spend them on rewards you set for yourself.

Features

  • Create daily routines with checkbox or timer-based tracking
  • Earn points for every completed routine
  • Set up custom rewards and purchase them with your earned points
  • Track streaks to stay motivated
  • Earn bonus points for Perfect Days (completing all routines)
  • Level up RPG-style attributes like Focus, Health, and Creativity
  • Live Activities for timer countdowns on your lock screen
