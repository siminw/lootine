Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Overview

Lootine is designed with your privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device, and we do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data Storage

All data you create in Lootine — including routines, rewards, points, streaks, and purchase history — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers or cloud services that receive your data.

Data Collection

Lootine does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-up is required
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No cookies or similar technologies are used

Third-Party Services

Lootine does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data.

Children's Privacy

Lootine does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at lootine.app@gmail.com.
